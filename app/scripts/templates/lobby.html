How To Play

Bingio is played with a 5 x 5 game board, similar to Bingo. Each board tile has the name of a song and artist.

Songs will play at random and the player must guess which song is playing by clicking on the appropriate tile.

The objective of the game is to correctly guess all 5 songs in a single row, column, or diagonal.

The first player to do so is the winner.

Note: each player only has 3 chances to correctly guess tiles. After 3 bad guesses, that player loses the game.
